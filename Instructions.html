Game Concept

  • Play as a robber who is inflitaring a house full of dangerous guards and cameras!

Core Characters

  • Rob: The main character that you control.
  • Cameras: Rob must avoid being seen by any of these cameras, or Rob dies and the level resets.
  • Guards: Guards are constantly moving around the level; if Rob is seen by any of these moving guards -- Rob dies and the level resets.

Instructions

  • Avoid being detected by cameras and guards (stay out of there line of sight)
  • Progress down the level (which is organized into different floors) by picking locks to open doors.
  • One you have opened a door, it acts as a portal (you enter one door, and are transported to a different open door.
  • Escape the level by sucessfully reaching the exit door (which is clearly indicated.)

Game Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys for basic movement (left, right, up down)
  • Click on 'Q' to lockpick a locked door
  • Click on 'S' on the down-arrow to enter an opened door
